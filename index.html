Great Blue Heron

Great Blue Herons breed in colonies of a few to several hundred pairs, with both parents taking turns incubating the eggs for 4 weeks.

  • Scientific Name: Ardea herodias
  • Average Length: 3 - 4.5 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Habitat: Wetland

A large bird, with a slate-gray body, chestnut and black accents, and very long legs and neck. In flight, the bird looks huge, with a six-foot wingspan.
